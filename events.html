Memorial Day

Date: 08/05/2020

This coming Friday, we are going to host a live broadcast of a memorial for all the people around the world that have died as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic. We will always be here for each other. In this world of technology, even though we are forced to stay at home, we - your family, friends and all other loved ones - are here for you and are there if you need us. You are not alone.

Human Day

Date: 09/05/2020

If you're reading this, you're alive. Let's come together in this time of strife and struggles and celebrate that we are all here. To those who have lost loved ones, we'll keep in mind and hope that you'll be able to get through the pain and the grief and that you never forget those who were so important in your life that you've remembered them even though they're gone.

#werehereforyou #werehereforeachother #lovingmeandlovingyou #COVIDisnttheend #welivetogetherwelovetogether


Forum for Improvement

Date: 11/05/2020

Mzamomtsha Primary School is proud to declare that we will be hosting a forum for anyone that wants to share anything about COVID-19 or ask any questions so that we all can become informed together. If you have any questions, you can post them on Twitter and tag them as #coronaaintamystery. Everyone's welcome, so please join us. COVID-19 has hit us all hard, and while we are happy to help gain knowledge, we would also like our students to learn. If anyone is willing to donate to our solidarity fund, we would love it.

#letssharetogether #itsforusall #COVIDcantbeatme #werehereforeachother #COVIDsgotnothingonme
