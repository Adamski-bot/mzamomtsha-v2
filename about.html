About Us

Our mission at Mzamomtsha Primary is to promote a love of learning and of life. While we truly believe that knowledge is power, we - as a school - also recognise that it requires a lot more than knowledge to create a generation that will surpass their predecessors. Because of this, we have chosen to nurture our students in the various ways of life. We hope to instill the core values of this school into our learners. Respect, tolerance, inclusion and excellence are values that we have and will always embody and share through our students and staff.

The vision of our school is simple: it is to create lifelong learners with the skills and mindset to pursue their aspirations and contribute to the world.

FAQ

Q: What do we plan to do with money received from donations?

A: As a school, there is always something that we could use money to obtain to make our learners live better. Many of our students come to school, hungry and sleepless because of home situations. With the money received from donations, one of our most important purchases would be that of food as well as that of staff, which will allow the quality of our learners' learning and education to improve.

Q: What facilities do we currently have?

A: At the moment, the Mzamomtsha Primary School has limited classrooms which prevents us from expanding to include high school classes. We also lack teachers that are capable of teaching our classes.
